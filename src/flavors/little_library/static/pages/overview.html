About

Hello! Welcome to our little app that is plotting the locations of Little Libraries in Madison.

This is a simple mission: try to scout out prospective locations for new libraries. While we're at it, it will be helpful to add the locations for existing libraries as well.

This app is brought to you by HackingMadison

Read more about Little Libraries at littlefreelibrary.org

Read more about Shareabouts at shareabouts.org, or head to Shareabouts on github. The project is open source, and we welcome your involvement. You can run Shareabouts on DotCloud, Heroku, and similar.

Need help? Email us at team@hackingmadison.org
